    6. One kind of list under another kind of list

    We can use Unordered List under an Ordered List and an Ordered List under an Unordered List, if it is necessary.

    Ordered under Unordered

    • Apple
      1. Gala Apple
      2. Fuji Apple
    • Orange

    Unordered under Ordered

    1. Apple
      • Gala Apple
      • Fuji Apple
    2. Orange
